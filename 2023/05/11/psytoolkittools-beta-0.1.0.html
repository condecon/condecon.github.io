    psytoolkittools 0.1.0-beta Release

    I am excited to announce the first beta release of my psytoolkittools-R package! This version gives a first preview on the upcoming features and is meant for public testing. I am still working on some features and improvements, but I wanted to give you a sneak peek of what’s coming.

    The psytoolkittools-R package is designed to help researchers analyze their data more efficiently. With this package, you can easily integrate survey and experiment data from you PsyToolkit experiments and surveys. Also, some other useful utilities are available such as to recode your key responses and statuses.

    I encourage you to download the beta version and give it a try. Your feedback is important to me and will help me make the final release even better.

    Thank you for your support!
